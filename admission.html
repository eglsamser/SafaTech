Polytechnic Admission

Best for students after 10th who want technical diploma education.

Required Qualification 10th pass with Science / Maths. For lateral entry, 12th Science or ITI may be required.
Entrance Exam State Polytechnic Entrance JEECUP , Exam form coming Every Year January , Exam conduct on May and Counselling Process Start on June

B.Tech Admission

For students who want Engineering career in CS, IT, Civil, Electrical, Mechanical etc.

Required Qualification 12th with required subjects, minimum 45% marks; 40% for SC/ST or Diploma for lateral entry admission.
Entrance Exam JEE Main for main UPTAC counselling. In special counselling, CUET UG may also be considered if seats remain, Direct admission as per rules.
Get Guidance
💻

M.Tech Admission

For students who want Engineering career in CS, IT, Civil, Electrical, Mechanical etc.

Required Qualification B.E. / B.Tech / MCA or related degree as per branch requirement
Entrance Exam CUET PG paper depends on branch. Example: CSE / IT / AI / Cyber Security mapping uses CUET PG paper codes like SCQP09.
Get Guidance
