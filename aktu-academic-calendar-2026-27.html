Important Notes for Students & Institutes

These instructions are not optional suggestions. They are operational requirements stated in the university calendar.

Minimum Teaching Hours

Institutes must complete the minimum teaching hours prescribed in university ordinances. Directors or principals must arrange extra classes on weekends or holidays, online or offline, whenever required.

Two Class Tests Are Compulsory

Every semester must include two class tests after completing one-third and two-thirds of the syllabus. Makeup tests must be provided for genuine absence, with the timing governed by the circular.

AMS Attendance Is Mandatory

Institutes must regularly submit attendance through the University Attendance Monitoring System (AMS). Students below the required attendance may be detained and barred from university examinations.

Leave for Evaluation Duty

Faculty assigned evaluation work during vacation are entitled to earned leave according to the rules, along with duty leave for other examination work assigned by the University.

Lost Syllabus Must Be Recovered

Institutes must compulsorily cover the syllabus lost by newly admitted third-semester B.Tech and B.Pharm students through extra classes in the early morning, late evening, Sundays or holidays.

Frequently Asked Questions

Direct answers to the dates AKTU students are most likely to search for.

When do AKTU odd semester exams start in 2026?

Odd-semester theory examinations for most programs start on 24 November 2026 and continue through 27 December 2026.

When will AKTU semester results be declared?

The odd-semester result is scheduled for 15 January 2027. The even-semester result is scheduled for 30 June 2027.

When do AKTU classes resume after vacation?

Classes for III, V, VII and IX semesters are scheduled to resume on 13 July 2027.

When will AKTU first-year classes begin in 2026?

First-year classes begin on 1 August 2026 for programs other than B.Pharm. First-year B.Pharm classes begin on 1 September 2026.
