AKTU Admission Process Through UPTAC

Understand AKTU admission process, UPTAC counselling, required entrance exams, document upload, choice filling and final reporting.

Home / Blog / AKTU Admission Process Through UPTAC

AKTU admission is mainly completed through Uttar Pradesh Technical Admission Counselling (UPTAC). For most courses, AKTU does not conduct a separate university-level entrance exam like old UPSEE/UPCET. Admission is generally based on national-level entrance exam scores like JEE Main, CUET UG, CUET PG and NATA, followed by UPTAC counselling.

Complete Process Step by Step

1

Select Your Course

First decide your course such as B.Tech, B.Arch, BBA, BCA, B.Pharm, MBA, MCA or any other professional course according to your qualification and career goal.

2

Check Required Entrance Exam

Check which entrance exam is required for your selected course. B.Tech admission is generally through JEE Main or CUET, BBA/BCA/B.Pharm and other UG courses through CUET UG, MBA/MCA through CUET PG, and B.Arch through NATA.

3

Fill Entrance Exam Form

Before AKTU counselling, students must fill the required entrance exam form with required subjects. Entrance exam forms generally come before the counselling process, so students should check official exam websites regularly.

4

Appear in Entrance Exam

After filling the entrance form, appear in the required exam such as JEE Main, CUET UG, CUET PG or NATA. Your admission chance depends on your entrance exam score, rank and counselling choices.

5

Check UPTAC Registration

After entrance exam result, UPTAC registration usually starts around May to June. Students must register on the official UPTAC portal to participate in AKTU counselling.

6

Complete One-Time Registration

UPTAC registration is usually a one-time process. If you miss the registration window, you may not be allowed to participate in later counselling rounds, so do not wait for the last date.

7

Pay Counselling Fee

During registration, students have to pay the counselling registration fee. The fee amount may change, so always check the official notification before payment.

8

Upload Documents

Upload required documents like marksheets, photo, signature, entrance exam scorecard, category certificate if applicable, domicile certificate if required and other documents carefully.

9

Document Verification

After uploading documents, the counselling authority verifies your documents. If any mismatch or error appears, correct it within the given time.

10

Choice Filling and Locking

After document verification, fill your preferred colleges and courses. Arrange choices carefully according to branch, college ranking, location, fee, placement and your career goal.

11

Seat Allotment

Seats are allotted in counselling rounds according to entrance exam rank, category, seat availability and the choices filled by the student.

12

Accept Seat

If a seat is allotted, pay the seat acceptance fee and choose your willingness option such as Freeze, Float or Withdraw according to your decision.

13

Report to Allotted College

After seat confirmation, report to the allotted college with original documents, allotment letter and required college fee to complete final admission.

14

Start Your Classes

After completing admission, attend classes regularly, focus on academics, practical skills, projects, internships and placement preparation.

Important Points

  • AKTU admission is mainly done through UPTAC counselling.
  • Old UPSEE/UPCET separate entrance exam is generally not conducted for most courses.
  • B.Tech admission is generally based on JEE Main or CUET score.
  • BBA, BCA, B.Pharm and other UG courses are generally based on CUET UG.
  • MBA and MCA admission is generally based on CUET PG.
  • B.Arch admission is generally based on NATA.
  • UPTAC registration usually starts after entrance exam result, mostly around May to June.
  • Registration is very important because it is usually a one-time process.
  • Choice filling must be done carefully because seat allotment depends on your filled choices.
  • Document mismatch can create admission problems, so upload correct documents only.
  • Always check college details like branch, fee, placement, location and facilities before locking choices.
  • Do not wait for the last date of registration, document upload or choice locking.
  • Dates, fees, eligibility and counselling rules can change, so students must always check the official AKTU/UPTAC notification before applying.
Note: Admission dates, fees, eligibility and official rules can change every year. Always confirm from the official counselling or university website before final admission decision.
