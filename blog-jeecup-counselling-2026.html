JEECUP Counselling 2026-27 Overview

This page is made for students who want Polytechnic admission through JEECUP counselling in 2026-27.

JEECUP counselling is the online admission process for Polytechnic colleges in Uttar Pradesh. After the entrance result, qualified candidates fill college and branch choices. After seat allotment, the candidate chooses Freeze or Float, pays the required fee, completes document verification and reports to the allotted college.

Important: Rank, cutoff and branch chances are expected guidance only. Final seat depends on category, branch, college, number of seats, counselling round, student preference and official JEECUP rules.

For 10th Passed Students

Polytechnic is normally a 3-year diploma course after Class 10th.

For 12th / ITI Students

Eligible students may get lateral entry/direct 2nd year admission as per JEECUP group and rules.

For Better Choice Filling

Fill more colleges and branches. Do not fill only 2-3 choices, otherwise allotment chance can reduce.

Complete JEECUP Counselling Process Step by Step

Follow these steps carefully to avoid seat cancellation.

1

Login on JEECUP Admission Portal

Open the official counselling portal and login using your application number/login ID and password. Keep your mobile number active because OTP or important messages may come.

2

Choice Filling

Select your preferred colleges and branches. Add maximum suitable options according to your rank, category, district, fee, placement, branch demand and distance from home.

3

Choice Locking / Final Submission

After checking all choices, lock/final submit the choices before the last date. Wrong order of choices can affect your allotment.

4

Seat Allotment Result

JEECUP releases seat allotment. You will see college name, branch and allotted category. Download/print the allotment details.

5

Choose Freeze or Float

Choose Freeze if you are satisfied and want to confirm the seat. Choose Float if you want to keep the seat but try for a better higher preference choice in next round.

6

Pay Security + Counselling Fee

Pay the required fee online through the portal within the deadline. If payment is not done on time, the seat may be cancelled.

7

Document Verification

Freeze candidates must visit the allotted district help centre for document verification. Carry all original documents and photocopies.

8

Download Provisional Admission Letter

After verification, download the provisional admission letter and keep copies for reporting at institute.

9

Report to Allotted College

Visit the allotted institute with original documents, admission letter, fee receipt and college fee. Complete final admission formalities before the deadline.

JEECUP Counselling Fee, Seat Acceptance Fee, Freeze & Float

Understand the difference before selecting your option.

Freeze Option

Choose Freeze when you want to confirm the allotted college and branch. After Freeze, you must complete document verification and report to the institute as per deadline.

Float Option

Choose Float when you want to keep the present allotted seat but also want a better seat in the next round according to your higher preference choices.

Fee Type Amount Mode Important Point
Seat Acceptance Fee ₹3000 Online Paid after allotment within deadline.
Counselling Fee ₹250 Online Paid with seat acceptance/security fee.
Total Portal Payment ₹3250 Online Keep payment receipt safe.
If you do not pay fee, do not verify documents or do not report to the allotted institute within time, your seat can be cancelled. Always check the latest official notice before payment and reporting.

Which Branch is Best in Polytechnic?

Branch selection should be based on interest + job scope + future study plan.

Computer Science Engineering

Very High Demand

Best for coding, software, web development, app development, artificial intelligence, data and IT jobs. Choose if you like computers and programming.

Information Technology

Very High Demand

Good for networking, software support, web, database, IT services and private sector jobs. Similar career path to CSE.

Electrical Engineering

High Demand

Evergreen branch for power, maintenance, solar, electrical industry, government and private sector technical jobs.

Mechanical Engineering

High Demand

Useful for manufacturing, auto sector, machines, workshop, production, CNC/VMC and industrial jobs.

Civil Engineering

Moderate to High

Good for construction, site engineering, government projects, contractor work, drawing, surveying and estimation.

Electronics Engineering

Technical Scope

Good for electronics devices, embedded systems, automation, communication and industrial maintenance.

Best practical advice: Do not choose only by trend. Choose CSE/IT if you can learn coding. Choose Electrical/Mechanical/Civil if your interest is core engineering and field/industry work.

Best Choice Filling Strategy for Government College

Correct choice filling can improve allotment chances.

  • Put your dream colleges and branches at the top, even if rank is slightly low.
  • After top colleges, add realistic government colleges according to your rank range.
  • Do not fill only CSE/IT if your rank is not strong. Add Electrical, Mechanical and Civil also.
  • Fill colleges in nearby districts like Jaunpur, Varanasi, Prayagraj, Azamgarh, Bhadohi, Sultanpur and Lucknow if suitable.
  • Check hostel, distance, fees, branch availability and placement before final choice locking.
  • Keep screenshots/PDF of submitted choices and payment receipt.
  • After allotment, do not miss Freeze/Float, fee payment and document verification dates.

For 1 - 15,000 Rank

Focus on top government colleges and high-demand branches. Add CSE/IT, Electrical and Mechanical in preference order.

For 15,000 - 50,000 Rank

Use a balanced list. Include more government colleges, nearby colleges and multiple branches.

For 50,000+ Rank

Participate in all rounds, fill many choices and keep a private college backup ready.

For Category Students

Use correct category certificate. Wrong or expired certificate can create problems during verification.

Common Mistakes During JEECUP Counselling

Avoid these mistakes to protect your seat.

  • Waiting for the last date of choice filling or fee payment.
  • Filling very few choices and missing realistic government college options.
  • Wrong order of college and branch preferences.
  • Confusion between Freeze and Float option.
  • Not downloading allotment letter, fee receipt and provisional admission letter.
  • Going to document verification without original documents.
  • Not reporting to allotted institute before deadline.
  • Using wrong mobile number/email ID and missing important updates.

Government College के लिए Best Choice Filling Strategy

Correct choice filling allotment chances improve कर सकती है।

  • Dream colleges और branches को top पर रखें, भले rank थोड़ा low हो।
  • Top colleges के बाद rank range के according realistic government colleges add करें।
  • Rank strong न हो तो केवल CSE/IT न भरें। Electrical, Mechanical और Civil भी add करें।
  • Suitable हो तो Jaunpur, Varanasi, Prayagraj, Azamgarh, Bhadohi, Sultanpur और Lucknow जैसे nearby districts add करें।
  • Final choice locking से पहले hostel, distance, fees, branch availability और placement check करें।
  • Submitted choices और payment receipt का screenshot/PDF safe रखें।
  • Allotment के बाद Freeze/Float, fee payment और document verification dates miss न करें।

1 - 15,000 Rank

Top government colleges और high-demand branches पर focus करें। CSE/IT, Electrical और Mechanical preference order में add करें।

15,000 - 50,000 Rank

Balanced list use करें। More government colleges, nearby colleges और multiple branches include करें।

50,000+ Rank

All rounds में participate करें, many choices fill करें और private college backup ready रखें।

Category Students

Correct category certificate use करें। Wrong या expired certificate verification में problem create कर सकता है।

JEECUP Counselling में Common Mistakes

Seat protect करने के लिए इन mistakes से बचें।

  • Choice filling या fee payment की last date का wait करना।
  • बहुत कम choices भरना और realistic government college options miss करना।
  • College और branch preferences का wrong order रखना।
  • Freeze और Float option में confusion होना।
  • Allotment letter, fee receipt और provisional admission letter download न करना।
  • Original documents के बिना document verification के लिए जाना।
  • Deadline से पहले allotted institute में report न करना।
  • Wrong mobile number/email ID use करना और important updates miss करना।
