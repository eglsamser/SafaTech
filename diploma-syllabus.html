Diploma syllabus questions

Which syllabus PDFs are available?
One common first-year PDF and five second-year branch PDFs are available: Computer Science, Civil, Electrical, Mechanical Automobile and Mechanical Production. The final-year section is shown as an update notice until a verified BTEUP final-year PDF is available.
Which semesters are inside the first-year PDF?
The common first-year document contains the first and second semesters.
Which semesters are inside each second-year PDF?
The second-year branch documents cover the third and fourth semesters.
Is the final-year syllabus available?
BTEUP has changed the final-year syllabus this year. The page shows an update notice for the third-year/final-year section and will be updated after the verified 5th and 6th semester syllabus PDF is available.
Are these PDFs an official substitute for institute confirmation?
No. Students should verify the applicable session, electives, exam scheme and later revisions with their institute or the relevant Uttar Pradesh technical education authority.
